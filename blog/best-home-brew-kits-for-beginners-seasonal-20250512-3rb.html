Seasonal Brewing Tips for Beginners

Summer Brews

  • Light & Refreshing: Wheat beers, pale ales, or fruit-infused brews
  • Brew Tip: Ferment in a cool, dark place to avoid off-flavors

Fall Brews

  • Warm & Spiced: Pumpkin ales, amber beers, or Oktoberfest lagers
  • Brew Tip: Add spices during bottling for aroma

Winter Brews

  • Rich & Bold: Stouts, porters, or spiced winter warmers
  • Brew Tip: Use darker malts for depth and warmth

Spring Brews

  • Crisp & Floral: Saisons, IPAs, or honey blondes
  • Brew Tip: Dry-hop for fresh, aromatic notes
