Why Choose a Lager Home Brew Kit?

Lagers are distinct from ales due to their fermentation process, which occurs at cooler temperatures and typically takes longer. This results in a clean, smooth flavor profile with a crisp finish. To brew a great lager, you'll need a kit that supports temperature control, provides quality ingredients, and offers clear instructions.
